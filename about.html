Manikanth Sharma - Technical Writer

Intro

With extensive experience in Technical Documentation across diverse industries—including aerospace, mechanical, banking, and manufacturing, I am a seasoned Technical Writer, Illustrator, and Reviewer showcasing expertise in both Hardware and Software Documentation. I am experienced in creating detailed User Guides, System Guides, SOPs, Operating and Maintenance Manuals, etc. with a profound ability to convey complex technical information clearly and precisly.

Having worked in various sectors, I bring a versatile approach to Technical Writing, adeptly adapting to industry-specific requirements and technical nuances. My role and experience as an Illustrator further enhance the documentation, allowing for the creation of complex diagrams and visual aids that complement the content.

I am always ready to embrace new challenges and tackle adversities, always striving to achieve new heights in technical writing. My dedication to excellence and continuous improvement drives the ability to deliver exceptional documentation solutions that meet the highest standards of quality and usability.
